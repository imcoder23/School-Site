School Calendar

Daily Routine & Time Table
Day Scholars:

Sunday to Thursday---->9:30AM - 3:30PM (Lower Sec/Sec)

Friday----------------------->9:30AM - 1:00PM

Sunday to Thursday------>10:00AM-1:00PM

Day Borders:

Students Should join the school at 6:30AM & remain upto 6:00PM

Semi-Day Boarders:

Students should join the school at 6:30AM to 3:30PM or 9:30AM to 6:00PM. followed by Morning Tuition class or Evening Home Work Class.

Boarders:

Starts at 6:00AM followed by morning PT,Tuition class,Day teaching session,Homework class,Evening study.

9:30PM is the bed time.

Special Tuition

In the morning there will be extra coaching and tuition classes for the residential students.These facilities can be provided to a day scholars students too.

Holidays:

School will be closed on every Saturday and on other public holidays.

Fees:

1):Monthly school fees will be charged for the period for the period of twelve months.

2):Payment should be made prior to 7th of every month.

3):Late payment of dues will be charged 5% in the round figure as the late fee.

Progress Report:

1):A monthly report,covering the students' performances will be sent to their guardians/parents,at the end of each month.

2):After completion of each academic term,every parent will be requested to see the students' progress reports and their records files,thoroughly.

Promotion:

To secure a promotion to the higher class,every student should pass in the final examination of the academic year.20% marks will be added from each terminal examination.

Text Books & Stationary

Every student should have a set of text books & stationary as per the requirement and pattern laid down by the school;which can be purchased at the counter of the school or prescribed book stall.

Mess:

1):A scientific mess facility has been adopted in the school.

2):Lunch,Tiffin
