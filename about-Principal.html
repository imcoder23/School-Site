From Principal's Pen

Mrs.Sangeeta Hamal

Mount Everest Education Foundation

Mount Everest Higher Secondary School was established in the 2046(1990).The founder of the school believe that education is the fundamental right of a citizen. They are against commercialisation in education.Thus,this school possesses a social and humanitarian theme with the objective to provide quality education to the students. This school also emphasises on ideal and moral life style of the teachers and students.

Mount Everest Education Foundation is fully non-governmental institution devoted towards the educational activities.

This school has completed a glorious history of 24 years. At present,this school is running the academic programmes from Nursery to Secondary levels.This school has been developed as a well academic institution in Bhaktapur district.Even in a short span of time,the school has been able to make sufficient satisfactory progress due to hard work of the related people.

The objectives of the school are as follows:

-To provide scientific education to meet the demand of the 21st century in academic career.

-To develop the students' moral character,physique and mind to enable them to become good and useful citizens.

-To meet the nationals standards of secondary levels education leading to centralize.

-To provide quality education to the students up to secondary level.

-To produce capable,efficient and qualified students in different fields.

-To contribute to the academic excellence of the nation in general and Bhaktapur district in particular through private institution.

-To fulfil the academic aspiration of the local people at affordable financial cost.

The school had adopted the following strategies to fulfil the objectives:

-The introduction of scientific education.

-English as a medium of instruction and communication.

-Qualified, well experience and dedicated teaching staff.

-Strict standard of teachers' and students' discipline.

-Sufficient emphasis on extra curricular activities to ensure the all-round development of the students.

-Providing improved teaching materials including English medium text books,audio-visual,equipments,computers,library,laboratory,etc.

Gradually,the school has changed its condition through hard struggle to bring it to the present condition. At present (after a period of 24 years), the school can be considered as a well-established educational centre.It has been a matter of pride for us to be introduced as a teacher or a student of this school.

We have been achieving very good results in the SLC examinations since 2053. In respect of co-curricular and extra curricular activities,our students have participated in different competitions and won various national and international awards.

the School Family has special identify and quality. We call it the Mount Everest Culture. This culture includes the co-operation among teachers-teachers,students-students and teachers and students. No member of the school family overlooks any work of problem lying in front of him/her.

We believe in work not in words.

We have good harmony, unity in action and unity in direction. It is the key to success of Mount Everest Education Foundation.
